## How to Order

Due to the artisan nature of my products, I can only build so many in a month that I am truly happy with the look and finish of. Because of this, I operating on a monthly drop, first come, first served basis. 

A new selection of unique items will be ‘dropped’ on the first weekend of every month (excluding holidays). Please see the [Monthly drop](./months-drop.html) page to see what's availible

If you would like to purchase one, please send me an email with the item reference, your name and full address, and I will reserve the item for you and get back to you with a quote. I will reserve the item for a maximum of 5 days and a reminder will be sent out after 3 days. After that, the quote is void and the item will be released back to the website, open to another buyer. 

The full and final price including delivery to UK mainland excluding highlands will be shown transparently below each item on the Drops page. I may have to charge an additional fee for delivery to the Scottish highlands, British isles and northern Ireland due to the additional logistics considerations and costs. 

In the unfortunate even that two of you request the same item, I will reserve it for the first person who has contacted me and notify the second that it has unfortunately been already reserved.
